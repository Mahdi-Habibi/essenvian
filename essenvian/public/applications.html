Applications

We support product categories with fragrance experiences that balance performance, distinctiveness, and consumer relevance. Below are typical application areas and example use-cases where we help clients achieve their goals.

Fine Fragrances

Luxury, niche, and mainstream fragrance concepts shaped by creative storytelling and technical precision. We develop concentrated perfumes, flanker sets, and seasonal scent families.

Personal Care

Tailored scent systems for soaps, lotions, haircare, and beauty rituals. Our work includes stability testing, skin-safe formulations, and masking solutions.

Home Care

Long-lasting fragrance solutions for fabric care, household cleaning, and air care experiences. We optimize volatility and delivery for sustained consumer perception.

Example Use-Cases

  • Positioning a premium cream with a signature soft-leather floral accord.
  • Designing a wash-care scent system that survives tumble drying.
  • Creating an immediate consumer-recognizable launch accord for a niche perfume.

Creativity through science — fragrance & sensory design for brands.
