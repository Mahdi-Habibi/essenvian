Engagement model

A structured path from brief to launch

We work as an extension of your product team. Every engagement begins with clear success criteria and concludes with documentation suitable for manufacturing handoff.

01

Brief & Insight

Market positioning, consumer cues, competitive mapping, and regulatory boundaries are defined before creative work begins.

02

Concept Families

Accord directions are developed as coherent families, enabling informed selection rather than isolated samples.

03

Formulation

Selected directions are refined for performance in your base, with attention to stability, cost, and sustainability goals.

04

Validation

Sensory evaluation and application testing confirm consumer-facing quality before scale-up recommendations.
