About ESSENVIAN

ESSENVIAN is a specialist fragrance and sensorial design studio that partners with global brands to create purposeful olfactory experiences. Combining R&D, material expertise and consumer insight, we design scents that elevate product storytelling.

Our Mission

To bring clarity and creativity to fragrance development while prioritizing safety and sustainability. We aim to make scent a strategic asset for product teams.
