About

A specialist studio for purposeful olfactory design

Essenvian is a fragrance and sensory design studio based in Grasse, France. We partner with global brands to create olfactory experiences that strengthen product strategy, storytelling, and in-use performance.

Studio

Creativity governed by scientific rigour

Our practice unites formulation expertise, material knowledge, and consumer insight. We treat fragrance as a strategic asset — one that must be designed with the same discipline applied to packaging, claims, and product architecture.

Working from Grasse places us within a heritage of aromatic craft while operating with contemporary methods: documented briefs, measurable criteria, and clear handoff to manufacturing partners.

Mission

Clarity, creativity, safety, and sustainability

We aim to make scent a dependable instrument for product teams — distinctive where it must be, restrained where it should be, and always accountable to safety and environmental expectations.

Clarity

Every recommendation is tied to a brief, a consumer cue, or a technical constraint. Ambiguity is reduced through structured options and documented decisions.

Creativity

Distinctive olfactive character remains essential. We pursue originality within frameworks that protect brand coherence and commercial viability.

Safety

Regulatory awareness and responsible use of materials are integral to development, not appended after creative selection.

Sustainability

We evaluate ingredient choices, substitution paths, and process implications so sustainability goals are practical rather than decorative.

Leadership

Multidisciplinary practice

Projects are led by specialists who cover formulation, sensory design, and innovation strategy, ensuring creative and technical accountability throughout the engagement.
